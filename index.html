On Deck

01 Mr. Puffy Western
A disfigured old man hires a down-on-his-luck pony express driver and his wayward teenage son to carry him and his stolen treasure west to California — what begins as a simple ride spirals into a nightmarish journey of death, buffalo, and madness.
02 Boshin War Period
As the sun sets on the samurai era, a drunken ronin escorts a Dutch prince across Japan as a year-long civil war tears the country in two.
03 Thomas Gendering Hamilton Drama
When one of his children dies of the Spanish flu, a rational doctor becomes consumed by séances and the occult — convinced he can reach the other side, blind to the truth that he is not searching for his child at all.
04 Visions of Gold Thriller
Chasing a vision of buried gold, a desperate vagrant drags an unlikely prophet five hundred miles into a heist that unravels faster than either of them can run.
05 Shantytown Surreal
A lonely Lima factory worker falls asleep on the bus and wakes up in a desert shantytown where every attempt to get home pulls him deeper into chaos.

About

Alex Fischman Cárdenas and Trout Cohen are a writer-director duo who met at a urinal on their first day at NYU. Since then, they've collaborated on narrative projects blending genre storytelling with surreal themes. Their work has earned Vimeo Staff Picks and screened at festivals such as Sundance, Clermont-Ferrand, Sitges, and AFI Fest.
